Programming Work

I dedicate a lot of my time to programming with Unity and C#, but I also have expertise in working with Unreal and C++.

Dragonfly - Duckhunt

A clone of the popular game Duckhunt made on a ASCII based game engine built from scratch using C++ and SFML.

3D First/Third Person Character Controller

Unity's rigidbody component was utilized for both physics and collision detection, along with capsule raycasting to assess collision response by incorporating a simplified version of the collide and slide algorithm.

Procedural Land Generation

Using Unity's Compute Shaders and meshes I implemented seed based SimpleX Noise to created terrain generation with procedurally placed trees.


Audio Work

I am skilled in working with Logic Pro X and other audio equipment, as well as playing various instruments, such as piano, drum set, percussion and guitar.

Music

A piece composed on the piano. It has 2 chords that repeat, those being Cmaj7 and D7. The melody was inspired from the works of Joe Hisaishi on the Studio Ghibli films.

Tranquility

A piece composed entirely on Logic Pro X. The piece is in G Harmonic Minor which gives it a dreadful feel. It depicts the way people attempt to escape death but ultimately fail.

Death's March

A piece composed in Logic Pro X. This piece represents a sense of waiting before a clash of heroes. Clashing time signatures gives this piece a sense of multiple voices.

Foreboding

The start of this piece was done on guitar, then moved to Logic Pro X where it was amplified and worked of further. The idea for this piece was two beings locked in a dance of life and death. This is the voice of death coming to terms with their part to play in the dance.
